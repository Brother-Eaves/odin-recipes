Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup packed Light Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Butter softened
  • 1/4 cup Sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 16 ounce package of Milk Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375. Grease cookie sheets.
  2. Into large bowl, measure all ingredients except for chocolate chips.
  3. Beat until well mixed
  4. Add choclate chips. Stir in well
  5. Drops by rounded teaspoon, 2 inches apart, on sheets.
  6. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until cookies are lightly browned.
  7. Remove cookies with spatula onto wire rack to cool. Enjoy
